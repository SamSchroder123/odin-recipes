Pizza

Description:

Pizza is a crowd-pleasing choice for busy weeknights and fun weekend dinners alike.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Dry yeast
  • Toppings of choice
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce

Steps:

  1. Make the dough
  2. roll out the dough
  3. Layer sauce
  4. Layer mozzarella
  5. Layer toppings
  6. Bake on high heat
  7. Voila!
